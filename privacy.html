Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026 · Effective immediately · ABN: 49 472 962 479

1. About This Policy

Voyeza is a travel social media platform operated by an Australian registered business (ABN: 49 472 962 479) based in Melbourne, Victoria, Australia.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data. It applies to all users of the Voyeza app and website worldwide.

We comply with:

  • Australian Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs)
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) — for users in the European Economic Area
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) — for users in California
  • Online Safety Act 2021 (Cth)

By using Voyeza you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Minimum Age

Voyeza is for users aged 16 and older, in compliance with the Online Safety Amendment (Social Media Minimum Age) Act 2024 (Cth). By creating an account you confirm you are at least 16. Accounts found to belong to users under 16 will be immediately deleted.

3. Information We Collect

Information you give us directly:

  • Account information: email address, display name, username, profile photo
  • Content you create: photos, videos, captions, comments, messages
  • Location data: only when you choose to tag a location on a post — we never track your background location

Information collected automatically:

  • Usage data: features you use, content you interact with, time spent in the app
  • Device information: device type, operating system version, app version, crash reports
  • Log data: IP address, access times, referring URLs

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information (e.g., health data, biometrics, political views, financial information, or government identifiers).

Analytics: Firebase automatically collects crash reports and usage events per Google's data processing terms (firebase.google.com/support/privacy).

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Display your content to other users according to your privacy settings
  • Operate the feed, passport, map, and messaging features
  • Send notifications you have opted into
  • Investigate and respond to reports of harmful content
  • Improve and maintain the app
  • Comply with legal obligations
We do not currently show advertising. In the future we may introduce advertising or sponsored content. If we do, we will update this policy and, where required by law, obtain your consent before using your data for advertising purposes. We do not sell your data.

We do not use automated decision-making or profiling that produces legal or similarly significant effects on you.

5. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area, we process your personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Contract: to provide the Voyeza service you signed up for
  • Legitimate interests: to improve the app, ensure safety, and prevent fraud
  • Legal obligation: to comply with applicable laws
  • Consent: where we ask for it specifically (such as optional notifications)

6. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With other users, according to your privacy settings (public posts are visible to all users; private account posts are visible only to approved followers)
  • With Google Firebase, which hosts our infrastructure (see Section 7)
  • With law enforcement or regulators when required by law, court order, or to protect safety
  • With the eSafety Commissioner when required under the Online Safety Act 2021 (Cth)
  • In the event of a business transfer or acquisition, with the acquiring entity (you will be notified)

We do not share your data with advertisers or data brokers.

7. Overseas Data Storage

Your data is stored on Google Firebase servers located in Iowa, United States (us-central1 region). By using Voyeza you consent to your personal data being stored and processed in the United States.

Google implements internationally recognised security standards including encryption in transit and at rest. Google LLC participates in the EU-US Data Privacy Framework.

For Australian users: we are required under the Australian Privacy Principles to disclose overseas transfers. For EEA users: transfers to the United States are made under Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) as provided by Google's data processing agreements.

8. Your Rights

All users:

  • Access the information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and all associated data (Settings → Account & Privacy → Delete Account)
  • Opt out of notifications via device settings

EEA users (GDPR):

  • Right to data portability
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time
  • Right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

California users (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know whether personal information is sold or disclosed (it is not)
  • Right to opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell it)
  • Right to deletion
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights
Categories of personal information collected: identifiers (email, username), internet or network activity (usage data, features used), and device information. We do not sell or share personal information for monetary value.
To exercise any of these rights contact us at voyeza.app@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Data Retention

We keep your data while your account is active. When you delete your account, all your data — posts, passport stamps, messages, and profile information — is permanently deleted within 30 days.

Some data may be retained longer if required by law or for legitimate safety purposes, such as records of reported harmful content and our responses.

10. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption for all data in transit and Firebase security rules to protect your data at rest. Your account is protected by authentication.

No system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but we take reasonable and appropriate measures to protect your information.

In the event of a data breach likely to result in serious harm, we will notify the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) and affected users as required by the Notifiable Data Breaches scheme.

11. Cookies and Tracking

The Voyeza app does not use cookies. Our website (voyeza.com) uses only essential technical cookies required for it to function. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies on our website.

12. Children's Privacy

Voyeza is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 16. If we become aware that a user is under 16 we will immediately delete their account and all associated data.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app. The date at the top shows when it was last updated. Continued use after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.
